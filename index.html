// 04 — DECISION ENGINE

Rule-Based Logic

Four rules evaluated in order. The first matching rule determines the active signal.

R1
Emergency Override
Emergency vehicle detected → immediate green signal. Overrides all other rules.
● STANDBY
R2
Maximum Traffic
Road with highest vehicle count receives priority. Direct density response.
● STANDBY
R3
Waiting Time
If any road exceeds 90s wait, it gains +20 priority bonus to prevent starvation.
● STANDBY
R4
Minimum Green Time
Current green must remain for ≥8s. Prevents rapid switching and vehicle confusion.
